Self-Serve Instructions

Follow these steps for the best results

1

Deposit Tokens

Deposit 8 tokens or more to start (minimum $8 = 8 tokens = 6 minutes)

2

SOAP - Wet Car

Turn dial to SOAP & use wand to wet car. Also spray off brush.

3

SUDS N' BRUSH / FOAM BRUSH - Scrub

Turn dial to SUDS N' BRUSH / FOAM BRUSH, then brush car generously with soap.

4

RINSE - Top to Bottom

Turn dial to RINSE. Use wand, rinse from top to bottom.

5

WAX

Turn dial to WAX. Use wand to apply protective wax coating.

⏱️ Need More Time?

When beep goes off, add additional tokens. Each token gives you 2 more minutes.

⚠️ Important Rules

  • RINSE BRUSH FIRST in case of sand/dirt from previous user
  • DO NOT use brush on dry surfaces, engines, or grease-covered areas
  • Remove all abrasive materials from brush
  • TRUCK OWNERS: Sweep debris into garbage before hosing bed
  • NO SPRAYING DEBRIS IN BAYS - Violators will be banned and police will be called for trespassing
