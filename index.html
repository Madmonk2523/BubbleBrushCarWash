Self-Serve Instructions

Follow these steps for the best results

1

Deposit Tokens

Deposit 6 tokens or more to start (minimum $8 = 6 tokens = 6 minutes)

2

SOAP - Wet Car

Turn dial to SOAP & use wand to wet car. Also spray off brush.

3

SUDS N' BRUSH - Scrub

Turn dial to SUDS N' BRUSH, then brush car generously with soap.

4

RINSE - Top to Bottom

Turn dial to RINSE. Use wand, rinse from top to bottom.

5

WAX - Avoid Windows

Turn dial to WAX. Use wand, avoid windows.
